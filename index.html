Feels Like Energy

Indulge in the silky, creamy and rich taste of Parle Agro’s dairy based flavoured drink, SMOODH. Available in 4 yummy flavors; Chocolate, Toffee Caramel, Coffee Frappé & Hazelnut Chocolate. SMOODH satisfies your craving for a quick energy boosting, sweet fix that is absolutely divine. Crafted combining the finest quality ingredients and goodness of milk, SMOODH is a deliciously wholesome and nourishing offering that will not just tingle your taste buds, but liven up your day in a healthy way.
